Welcome to myPlate, the new social network for connecting homegrown chefs to hungry people.


our Story:

After many years of real life testing and in the field experience, we at myPlate have realized that all the best food comes from the hard to find, unestablished places. Often from someone's home kitchen or food truck that you really just have to be "in the know" to know about, ya know?

Well, we wanted to make those places a little easier to find. Give them a spotlight so all the other food lovers like us can always stay full, and the ones that love to cook can stay in business. We present to you: myPlate.
